## What is AI Slides?

AI Slides is Neuro's intelligent presentation creation agent that automates research, content generation, and slide design. Transform your ideas into professional presentations with just a simple prompt.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Instant presentations" icon="bolt">
    Get complete, designed slides in minutes from just one prompt
  </Card>
  <Card title="Automated research" icon="magnifying-glass">
    AI researches your topic, analyzes sources, and compiles findings with proper citations
  </Card>
  <Card title="Multimedia integration" icon="images">
    Add images, videos, and audio through AI generation or web sources—perfectly sized
  </Card>
  <Card title="Design flexibility" icon="palette">
    Change styles and visuals using natural language—no design skills needed
  </Card>

## Quick start

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to AI Slides" icon="presentation">
    Click **"AI Slides"** at the bottom of Neuro homepage, or select it from the **"New"** menu in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Describe what you want to create" icon="message">
    Enter your topic, goal, or key message. You can also upload documents for AI to analyze and extract key insights.
    
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Sample prompts" icon="lightbulb">
        - "Create a 10-slide pitch deck for a sustainable fashion startup"
        - "Make a presentation about quantum computing for beginners"
        - "Build a quarterly business review with our sales data"
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Get your presentation" icon="check">
    AI Slides automatically researches, structures, designs, and generates a complete, visually compelling deck. Review, refine, and export as PDF, PPTX, or Google Slides.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key features

### <Icon icon="wand-magic-sparkles" /> Slides Templates

Use pre-designed templates to create presentations with consistent styles and layouts. The template library includes various design styles, topics, and language options.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse templates">
    Browse the template library to find a template that matches your needs
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select and customize">
    Select a template and enter your prompt describing the content you want to create
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate">
    AI Slides generates your presentation using the selected template's design
  </Step>
</Steps>

### <Icon icon="file-import" /> Document conversion

Import existing files and convert them to AI Slides format:

<CardGroup cols={4}>
  <Card title="PDF" icon="file-pdf" />
  <Card title="Excel" icon="file-excel" />
  <Card title="Word" icon="file-word" />
  <Card title="PowerPoint" icon="file-powerpoint" />
</CardGroup>

Simply upload your document, and AI Slides will analyze and extract key insights to create a new presentation.

### <Icon icon="eye" /> View and present

View and present your slides directly in a browser without downloading or exporting files. Changes made to your presentation are immediately reflected in the viewing window.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open viewer">
    Click **"View & Export"** in your presentation
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select view mode">
    Select **"Presentation View"** from the dropdown menu
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start presenting">
    Click **"Play Slides"** to start presenting
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips for best results

<Tip>
  **Be specific with your prompts**: Include details about your audience, purpose, and desired tone. For example: "Create a 15-slide technical presentation about cloud architecture for senior developers, with code examples and diagrams."
</Tip>

<Note>
  **Upload supporting materials**: If you have existing documents, data, or research, upload them to help AI Slides create more accurate and relevant content.
</Note>

<Warning>
  **Review AI-generated content**: Always review citations, facts, and figures before presenting to ensure accuracy and relevance to your specific context.
</Warning>
