---
title: "Will my personal data be deleted after I delete my account?"
description: "Learn what happens to your personal data when you delete your Neuro account."
---

When you delete your Neuro account, we will take steps to delete your content from our services. According to our Terms of Use, upon termination, we may delete your content stored on the services.

However, please be aware that we may need to retain certain personal information for a period of time for specific business and legal purposes. As stated in our Privacy Policy, we retain personal information to:

- Satisfy any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.
- Establish or defend legal claims.
- Prevent fraud.

We will only retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill these purposes. Once the retention period expires, your data will be securely deleted.

If you are part of a **Team Plan**, your data retention may be determined by your team's Owner or Administrator. Please contact them for specific details about your team's data retention policies.
