---
title: "Will Neuro publicly display or recommend projects, templates, or playbooks created by users without explicit consent?"
description: "Neuro will not publicly display your tasks without your consent."
---

No, Neuro will not publicly display or recommend your projects, templates, playbooks, or any other user-generated content without your explicit action to do so.

## **You Control Your Content**

Our Privacy Policy is clear: your content is made visible to others only **at your election**. This means you have full control over what you share.

For example, content may become visible to others if you:

- Explicitly use a "Share" feature within the platform.
- Choose to publish a project or template to a public gallery.

Unless you take a specific action to make your content public, it will remain private to your account.
