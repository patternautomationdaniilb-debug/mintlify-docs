We reserve the right to modify our [<u>Privacy Policy</u>](https://manus.im/privacy) at any time. Our commitment is to keep you informed about how we protect your privacy.

**How We Notify You**

When we make material changes to the Privacy Policy, we will notify you by:

- Updating the **"Last updated"** date at the top of the Privacy Policy document.
- Posting the new policy on our Service or other appropriate means.

Depending on the significance of the changes, we may also provide additional notice or request your consent.

We encourage you **to review our Privacy Policy periodically** to stay informed about our data practices. Your continued use of the Service after any changes signifies your acceptance of the new policy.
