**How We Handle Your Data**

We may use _aggregated_ or _de-identified_ information—data processed so it no longer identifies any individual—to improve our Services. This includes enhancing product performance, diagnosing issues, and preventing abuse.

We keep this information in de-identified form and will not attempt to re-identify it unless required by law.

For more details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
