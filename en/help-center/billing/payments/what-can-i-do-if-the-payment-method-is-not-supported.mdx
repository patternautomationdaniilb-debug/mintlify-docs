### **Our Commitment to a Seamless Payment Experience**

At Neuro, we strive to provide a seamless and secure payment experience for all our users. We understand that you may have a preferred payment method, and we are continuously working to expand our supported options. This article outlines our currently accepted payment methods.

### **Supported Payment Methods**

We currently accept a variety of payment methods to accommodate our global user base. These are processed securely through our payment partners.

| Category           | Supported Payment Methods                                   |
| :----------------- | :---------------------------------------------------------- |
| Credit/Debit Cards | Visa, Mastercard, American Express, JCB, China UnionPay,MIR |
| Digital Wallets    | Apple Pay, Google Pay, Alipay, Link                         |
| Direct Debit       | PayNow (for users in Singapore), SPB (Yookassa)             |
