# **Strategies to Optimize Credit Usage**

## **1. Combine Similar Questions**

Whenever possible, group related or similar questions into one task. By doing so:

- You can reduce the number of separate attempts that consume credits.
- Streamlined grouping allows for consolidated and efficient resolution.

Example: Instead of asking separate questions about formatting and saving a file, combine them into one comprehensive query.

Repeatedly asking variations of the same question can exhaust credits unnecessarily. Instead:

- Review the responses provided carefully before resubmitting similar queries.
- Make corrections to your inputs or instructions for better success rates.

Example: If a question does not yield the intended answer, refine it based on the initial response instead of rephrasing aimlessly.
