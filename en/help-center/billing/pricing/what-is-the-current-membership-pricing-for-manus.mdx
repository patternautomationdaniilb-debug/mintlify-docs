You can find our pricing through the Neuro -\> button left "Account" -\> "Plan" .

After entering the Neuro homepage on [patternautomation.com](http://patternautomation.com) , scroll down to the bottom of the page to view our product pricing.

Currently, Neuro offers Basic & Plus & Pro & Ultra plan options to meet the needs of different users. Whether you are an individual user or a team user, Neuro can provide you with efficient solutions.

**_\*Please note that Basic users only have access to North Star 1.0 Lite and Neuro Lite in Agent Mode, while Plus, Pro, Ultra users have access to North Star 1.0, North Star 1.0 Max, and Neuro Advanced in Agent Mode. All Plans has access to Chat Mode._**

Here are the detailed contents of each plan:

### **Basic Plan Price: \$0/month**

1. Access to Chat Mode and Neuro Lite in Agent Mode.
2. Features: Daily Refresh Credits: 0 credits provided daily for quickly answering simple questions. (soon will be more)
3. Concurrent Tasks: Supports 1 concurrent task.
4. Scheduled Tasks: Supports 0 scheduled tasks.
5. Use Cases: Suitable for individual users with low functional requirements to experience Neuro's basic features.

### **Plus Plan Price 1: Starting from \$9,9/month (Enjoy a 15% discount with annual billing)**

Basic users can upgrade to a membership for more credits. Members who need additional credits can switch to a higher plan.

1. Access to Chat Mode and North Star 1.0, Neuro Lite and Advanced in Agent Mode.
2. Features: Monthly Credits: Starting from 999 credits per month, supporting higher frequency usage needs.
3. Advanced Research: Supports advanced research on any topic.
4. Professional Deployment: Provides professional-level website deployment services.
5. Slide Generation: Generates slides with deep insights and a clear structure.
6. Research: Performs better in high-intensity tasks.
7. Task Quantity: Supports 2 concurrent tasks and 5 scheduled tasks.
8. Beta Feature Early Access: Experience the latest features in advance.
9. Use Cases: Suitable for users who need to conduct in-depth research and generate professional reports, helping to improve work efficiency.

### **Pro Plan Price 2: Starting from \$25/month with 7-day free trial (Enjoy a 15% discount with annual billing)**

Free users can upgrade to a membership for more credits. Members who need additional credits can switch to a higher plan.

1. Access to Chat Mode and North Star 1.0, Neuro Lite and Advanced in Agent Mode.
2. Features: Monthly Credits: Starting from 4,000 credits per month, supporting higher frequency usage needs.
3. Advanced Research: Supports advanced research on any topic.
4. Professional Deployment: Provides professional-level website deployment services.
5. Slide Generation: Generates slides with deep insights and a clear structure.
6. Wide Research: Performs better in high-intensity tasks.
7. Task Quantity: Supports 5 concurrent tasks and 10 scheduled tasks.
8. Beta Feature Early Access: Experience the latest features in advance.
9. Use Cases: Suitable for users who need to conduct in-depth research and generate professional reports, helping to improve work efficiency.

### **Ultra Plan Price: Starting from \$99 per seat/month (Enjoy a 15% discount with annual billing)**

1. Access to Chat Mode and North Star 1.0 and North Star 1.0 Max, Neuro Lite and Advanced in Agent Mode.
2. Target Users: Team users who need collaboration and advanced management features.
3. Features: Includes all features of the Ultra plan.
4. SSO (Single Sign-On): Provides more convenient login methods.
5. Data Training Opt-Out: Supports opting out of data training to protect privacy.
6. Team Usage Analytics: Provides analytical data on team usage.
7. Internal Access Control: Better management of team permissions.
8. Shared Slide Templates: Provides shared slide templates for teams to improve collaboration efficiency.
9. Use Cases: Suitable for enterprise or team users who need team collaboration, data privacy protection, and advanced management features.

Everything is subject to what is displayed on the actual pricing page.

**_\*Please note that Subscriptions and Plans could change and to check actual pricing go to Neuro AI_**

### **Troubleshooting:**

Question: What will happen to existing members under the new pricing plans?

- Existing member users will still auto-renew with unchanged prices and can upgrade or downgrade to new plans. The original plans will be renamed to new Plan, it doesn't affect their usage.
