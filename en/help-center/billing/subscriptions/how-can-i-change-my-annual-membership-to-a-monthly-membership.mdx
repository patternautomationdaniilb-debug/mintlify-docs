---
title: "How can I change my annual membership to a monthly membership?"
description: "Learn how to switch from an annual Neuro AI subscription to a monthly plan."
---

If you accidentally purchased an annual plan, please don't panic.

You can send your invoice or invoice screenshot (preferably including order number, receipt number, or payment email information) to us.

- If you meet our refund requirements:

  Our staff will process your refund, after which you can subscribe to our monthly subscription plan.
- If you do not meet our refund requirements:

  Once your subscription period has ended, you may subscribe to our monthly subscription plan.
