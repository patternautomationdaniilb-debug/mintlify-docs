# **You subscribed via Stripe or Google**

If you wish to request a subscription refund, please log in to the Manus account you want to refund. You may submit your invoice information, email address, and your request to our Help Centre AI chatbot, and we will process it according to your specific situation.

Please let us know the order you want to refund and the reason for the refund. Our team will respond to you as soon as possible.

If you meet the refund conditions, we will process the refund in accordance with our [<u>refund policy</u>](https://manus.im/terms). After the refund is processed, the refunded amount will be returned to your original payment account within 1-15 business days.

# **You subscribed via Apple Subscriptions**

For subscriptions made through Apple, refunds must be requested directly from Apple. You can apply for a refund from Apple through [<u>this Apple refund guide</u>](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT204084).
