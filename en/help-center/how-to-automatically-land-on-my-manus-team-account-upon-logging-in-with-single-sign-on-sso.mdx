**How It Works: A Step-by-Step Guide**

1. Initiate Login: Navigate to the login page and enter your email address.
2. SSO Authentication: After logging in with your credentials, user is brought to the SSO authentication page to complete authentication.
3. Automatic redirection: User will automatically land on the Neuro Team workspace upon successful SSO authentication.

_\*No additional steps are required from the user. The system handles the entire redirection process in the background._

**Troubleshooting**

If you are not automatically redirected to your team's workspace after logging in, please consider the following:

- Incorrect SSO Configuration: Your team's administrator should verify that the SSO configuration is correctly set up and that your user profile is properly mapped within the identity provider.
- Browser Cache Issues: Try clearing your browser's cache and cookies, as outdated session information can sometimes interfere with the redirection process.
