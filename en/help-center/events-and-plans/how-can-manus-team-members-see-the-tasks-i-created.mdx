---
title: "How can Neuro Team members see the tasks I created?"
description: "Learn how to share tasks with Team members in Neuro."
---

Neuro is fully committed to respecting user privacy. All tasks are designated as private, and team members do not have access to tasks created by other members. If you want to share it with others for viewing, you can follow the steps below.

Step 1: Click "**Share**" on the page.

Step 2: To let your Team members view your task, click "**Team access**", then "**Copy link**" on the page.(soon)

Step 2: To let the public view your task, click "**Public access**", then "**Copy link**" on the page.
