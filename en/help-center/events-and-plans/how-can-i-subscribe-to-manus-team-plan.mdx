title: "How can I subscribe to Neuro Team plan?"
description: "Learn how to subscribe to our Neuro Team plan and upgrade from individual to Team subscription in just 4 easy steps.  "
---

If you already possess a Neuro account and wish to acquire the Team version, you may proceed with the subscription by following the steps outlined below.

Step 1: Click your **Icon** at the top right of the page.

Step 2: Click **“Homepage”** on the page.

Step 3: Scroll down to see our **“Pricing”** page and click **“Get Team”**.

Step 4: You can now choose a subscription based on your needs.
