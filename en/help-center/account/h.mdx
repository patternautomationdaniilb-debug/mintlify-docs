title: "How can I delete my tasks?"
description: "Understand what happens to your data and important warnings about permanent deletion."
---

**Important Note:** Upon deletion, all data (space/website/files, etc.) contained within this task will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

1. Click "**More options**" on the page.

2. Click "**Delete**" on the page. (Note: Deletion will be irreversible.)
