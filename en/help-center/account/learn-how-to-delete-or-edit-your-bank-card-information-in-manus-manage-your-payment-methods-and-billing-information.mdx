---
title: "How can I delete my account?"
description: "Learn how to permanently delete your Manus Account through our step-by-step guide."
---

**_Important Note: _**_Once you delete your account, all of your data will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered._

Step 1: Click on your **Icon** at the top right of the page.

Step 2: Click "**Account**" on the page.

Step 3: Click the "**Edit profile**" on the page.

Step 4: Click "**Delete account**" on the page.

Step 5: Click the "**Send Code**" to send a verification code to your email. After entering the verification code, click "**Delete account**". (Note: Deletion will be irreversible)
