---
title: "How can I get a campus pass?"
---

Step 1: Click "**Campus**" at the bottom of the [<u>homepage</u>](https://manus.im/home).

Step 2: Click "**Search for eligible schools**" on the [<u>campus verification page</u>](https://manus.im/edu). Check if your school is included.

Step 3: Click "**Verify your edu email**" to verify your campus email.
