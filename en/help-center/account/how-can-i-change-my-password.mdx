---
title: "How can I change my password?"
description: "Learn how to change your Neuro password. Discover who can change passwords and the verification process for email-registered users."
---

Neuro only supports users who registered with regular email to change their password. If you signed with Google/Apple account, you cannot change your password since no password was set during your login process.

1. Click your **Icon** at the button left of the page.
2. Click "**Settings**" on the page.
3. Click "**General**" on the page.
4. Click "**Update password**" on the page.
5. Click "**Send Code**" to send a verification code to your email, then return to this page to enter the code for verification.
6. Enter the new password, then click "**Confirm**" to complete the password change process.
