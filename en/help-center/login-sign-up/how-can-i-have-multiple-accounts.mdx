---
title: "How can I have multiple accounts?"
description: "Learn about Manus' policy on multiple accounts."
---

To reduce the risk associated with your account, we do not recommend users having multiple different accounts. Currently, Manus supports synchronized login of the same account across multiple devices to meet users' needs for coordinated work on multiple devices.

Common related FAQs:

- Can I use the same account on multiple devices?
- What is the alternative to having multiple accounts?
- Does Manus support synchronized login?
- Can I work on multiple devices with one account?
- What are the risks of having multiple accounts?
- How can I do coordinated work on multiple devices?
