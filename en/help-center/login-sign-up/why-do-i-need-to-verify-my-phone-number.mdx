---
title: "Why do I need to verify my phone number?"
description: "Understand why Neuro requires phone number verification for email login."
---

If you log in with a Google/Apple account, you don't need to verify your phone number.

If you log in with your email, you must verify your phone number.

_Note_: To maintain system stability and prevent unauthorized bulk registrations and malicious activities, this verification process is necessary. We appreciate your understanding and cooperation.
