title: "What should I do if I forgot my registered email?"
description: "Learn how to recover your Manus account if you forgot your registered email."
---

We suggest trying your other emails. If you still cannot locate the correct email, and you have previously subscribed to Manus and have a payment record, you can provide us with your previous invoice number to help us find your email.
