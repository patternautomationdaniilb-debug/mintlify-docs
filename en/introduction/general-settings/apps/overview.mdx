## Troubleshooting

### App can't find data

**Check:**

- Does the app have access to that data source?
- Is the data in the location the app can see?
- Try a broader search query

**Solution:**

- Verify app permissions
- Reconnect the app (re-authenticate)
- Check data location

---

### Write action not working

**Check:**

- Does the app have write permissions?
- Have your credentials expired?
- Is the target system available?

**Solution:**

- Grant write permissions
- Re-authenticate the app
- Verify target system is accessible

---

### Slow response times

**Check:**

- Large amount of data to process?
- Network connection speed?

**Solution:**

- Make searches more specific
- Try during off-peak times
- Check app status
