Connectors allow Manus to access and control your other apps directly from your tasks.**Use cases:**

- Have Manus read and respond to your Gmail messages
- Let Manus schedule meetings in your Google Calendar
- Enable Manus to search and update your Notion databases

**Setup**You can set up your integrations from [**manus.im**](http://manus.im) using secure OAuth authentication. Each connector uses industry-standard OAuth protocols to ensure your data remains secure and private.
