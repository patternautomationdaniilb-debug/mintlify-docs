**Super Agent** is Genspark's autonomous AI assistant that thinks, plans, and acts to complete your everyday tasks.

- **One prompt does it all** — Research, content creation, data analysis, phone calls, emails, and beyond
- **World's first hybrid system** — Intelligently combines 30+ models, 150+ tools, and 700+ MCP integrations for optimal results on every task
- **Powered by specialized agents** — Coordinates Slides, Sheets, Doc, Designer, Developer, and more to handle complex workflows with endless possibilities

## **Quick Start**

Navigate to the Genspark homepage and enter your prompt in the central chat box, or select "Super Agent" from the "New" menu in the sidebar.

**What to ask:** Describe any task you need—research, content creation, data analysis, design, coding, communication, and more. Super Agent will coordinate the right agents and tools automatically.
