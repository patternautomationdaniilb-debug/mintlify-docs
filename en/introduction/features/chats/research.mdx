Neuro evaluates source credibility by considering:

- **Domain authority** — Established, reputable sources rank higher
- **Expertise** — Subject matter experts and industry leaders are prioritized
- **Recency** — Newer data is preferred for fast-moving topics

Because Neuro research is tied to actual web sources, it dramatically reduces hallucinations:

- Every statistic is backed by a source
- Claims are verifiable through provided citations
- Contradictions are flagged and explained
- Uncertain information is clearly marked

**Traditional AI Chatbot:**

**Neuro Research:**

> "The global AI market was worth \$500 billion in 2024" _(Cited: Statista, Fortune 500 Reports, and McKinsey Analysis)_

## Limitations & Considerations

### Research Quality Factors

While Neuro Research is powerful, understand these factors:

**Internet Availability**

- Research depends on web access
- Paywalled content may not be accessible
- Very new information (\< 24 hours) may not be indexed yet

**Source Selection**

- Popular sources are prioritized (good for consensus data)
- Niche sources may be harder to find
- You should always review citations for reliability

**Data Interpretation**

- Neuro interprets data accurately but isn't a subject matter expert
- Complex technical topics may need human review
- Domain expertise should validate key findings

**Regulatory & Compliance**

- Not a substitute for legal research or compliance review
- Always verify sensitive information with experts
- Regulatory information changes frequently

## FAQ: Research with Neuro

### How current is the research?

Neuro accesses the live web by using NeuroAI Coputer, so research is as current as publicly available information. Most data is updated daily or in real-time.

### Can Neuro research behind paywalls?

Limited access to paywalled content. Neuro can access publicly available abstracts, summaries, and free articles. For subscription-based databases, citations are provided but full content isn't accessible.

### How many sources does Neuro analyze?

For a typical research query, Neuro analyzes 50-100+ sources. For comprehensive research, it can analyze significantly more. The exact number depends on topic specificity and importance.

### Can I trust the citations?

Citations are automatically generated from sources Neuro accesses. However, you should always verify critical information, especially for legal, financial, or medical decisions.

### How long does research take?

Most research completes in 5-15 minutes. Very extensive research (20+ slides with comprehensive analysis) may take up to 30 minutes.

### Can I use research in confidential documents?

Yes. All research is conducted securely. Information is never shared unless you share it publicly.

### What if I need research for a specific industry or niche?

Neuro Research works across all industries. More niche topics may require additional time to gather sufficient sources, but results are comprehensive.

### Can Neuro verify conflicting information?

Yes. When Neuro finds conflicting data, it flags the contradiction and explains the discrepancy. This helps you understand nuance in data.
