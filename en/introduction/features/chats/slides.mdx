## Pro Tips

### Plan for Clean, Balanced Layouts

Want professional-looking slides? Plan your content structure before generating. Start with an outline and keep information volume consistent per slide. If a slide looks too crowded, split the information across multiple slides—you can always pause generation and adjust as you go.

**Try this approach:**

> Create a comprehensive outline first, determine content for each slide, and keep information volume consistent per slide. Wait for my confirmation before starting production.

### Keeping Your Style Consistent

To keep your slides looking cohesive, here are two approaches that work well:

**1. Sequential Creation**

Tell the AI to generate slides one at a time:

> Please create slides one by one, do not process in parallel

**2. Style Approval Workflow**

- Generate the first slide
- Review and approve the style
- Tell AI to match that exact style for the rest:

> Please create the first slide and wait for my approval of the style. Then create all remaining slides to match that exact style—same fonts, colors, spacing, and layout structure.

### Saving Credits

Use these tips to make the most of your AI credits:

- **Stop generation early:** If the output isn't what you want, stop it immediately instead of letting unused slides finish.
- **Use Advanced Edit for small changes:** Advanced Edit is free—use it for text, formatting, colors, or layout tweaks. Reserve AI generation for major revisions or new slides.
- **Plan before you generate:** Finalize your outline and structure first to avoid spending credits on unnecessary slides.
