---
title: "How can I share a task?"
description: "Share public task links with the support team, family, or friends in 4 easy steps for better assistance and collaboration (soon)."
---

If users encounter any difficulties during their task with Neuro or need assistance from the support team, they can share the public task link with the support team to get help. This is not mandatory, but having the task link allows the support team to provide more targeted suggestions and assistance.

Therefore, to better understand the conversation scenario for problem identification and troubleshooting, it is suggested to share the public task link by:

1. Click the share button in the top right area of the task.
2. Click the public access button.
3. Click the copy link button.
4. Send us the public access task link.

Besides, users can also share their tasks with their family or friends in this way, showcasing their usage process and experience with them.
