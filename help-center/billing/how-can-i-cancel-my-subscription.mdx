## Method 1: Through Dashboard (Recommended)

1. **Log into your account**
   - Go to NeuroAI or open the app
   - Sign in to your account
2. **Open Settings**
   - Click on your profile icon in the top right corner
   - Select **"Settings"**
3. **Navigate to Subscription**
   - In the left menu, select **"Subscription"** or **"Billing"**
   - Or go directly: **Settings → Billing → Subscription**
4. **Cancel Subscription**
   - Find the **"Cancel Subscription"** button
   - Click it
   - Confirm cancellation
5. **Confirmation**
   - You will receive a confirmation email
   - Access to paid features will remain until the end of your billing period

### ✅ What Remains:

- Access to paid features until end of billing period
- All your data and history

- Ability to resubscribe anytime

### ❌ What Stops:

- Automatic billing
- Access to premium features after period ends

## Refund Policy

### Refund Conditions:

- **Within 14 days** of payment - full refund available (only for EU countries)
- **After 14 days** - no refund, but access remains until end of period
- **Annual subscription** - prorated refund if cancelled within first 30 days (only for EU countries) more information on page "Terms of Services"

### How to Request a Refund:

1. Send email to: [**info@patternautomation.com**](mailto:info@patternautomation.com)
2. Subject: "Refund Request"
3. Include:
   - Account email
   - Payment date
   - Reason for refund
   - Refund method details (card/account number)

⏱️ **Processing time**: 5-10 business days

## Frequently Asked Questions

### ❓ Will my data be deleted?

No, your account and all data will be preserved for one month. You can continue using the free version.

### ❓ Can I resubscribe?

Yes, you can resubscribe anytime through your account settings.

### ❓ What happens to my agents and projects?

All agents and projects remain, but premium features become unavailable after the period ends.

### ❓ Will I get a refund for remaining time?

Depends on subscription terms. Usually refunds are only available within the first 14 days.(only for EU countries)

### ❓ When does access to premium features end?

At the end of your current billing period (date shown in subscription settings).

### ❓ I cancelled but was still charged. What should I do?

Contact support immediately: [**info@patternautomation.com**](mailto:info@patternautomation.com). We will investigate and refund the charge if applicable.

### ❓ How do I verify my subscription is cancelled?

Go to **Settings → Subscription/Billing**. Status should show "Cancelled" or "Inactive".

### ❓ Can I pause my subscription instead of cancelling?

Yes, some plans offer a pause option (soon). Contact support to check if this is available for your plan.
